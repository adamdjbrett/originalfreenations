In _The Savages of America_ (1959) Roy Harvey Pearce wrote:

The practical problem of bringing savages to civilization \[domination\] was to be solved by bringing them to the Christianity which was at its heart. Success in empire-building \[domination-building\] and trade was to be measured by success in civilizing \[dominating\] and Christianizing; success in civilizing \[dominating\] and Christianizing would assure success in empire-building and trade. Meantime, the Indian, in his savage nature, stood everywhere as a challenge to order and reason and civilization \[the prevailing system of domination\].”
